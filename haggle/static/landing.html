How It Works

Three steps to a fair deal.

1

Search what others paid

Type an item and city. See real prices reported by travellers, scraped from Reddit, and sourced from wholesale data — all in one place.

2

Get your negotiation script

Enter what the vendor said and their asking price. Get an AI-crafted counter-offer, a target price, and culture-specific haggling tips — instantly.

3

Pay fair, not minimum

See the local daily wage and how long the item takes to make. Haggle to a fair price that works for both sides — then share what you paid.
