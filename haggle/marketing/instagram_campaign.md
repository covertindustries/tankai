## Growth Tactics

1. **Reply to travel accounts** posting market photos — add value in comments
2. **Collab with travel creators** — offer "exclusive city price data" in exchange for a story mention
3. **User-generated content** — "Share what you paid + city = get featured"
4. **Tourism board DMs** — pitch a free partnership: they promote, we feature their city
5. **Reddit** — post in r/solotravel, r/travel, r/backpacking with the data (no hard sell)
6. **Instagram Lives** — "Ask a Haggler" — answer live questions about market prices
